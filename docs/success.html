Org-Only OAuth Consent Simulation

⚠️ This was a phishing simulation – no credentials were captured.

You just interacted with a real Microsoft login page and almost approved an app request. Attackers use this exact technique to gain access to your data.

What should you have noticed?

  • The app name was Microsoft Security Portal – sounds official but is not a verified Microsoft app.
  • The permissions requested included access to your profile and maintaining access to data.
  • Attackers rely on trust in familiar branding and URLs. The login page was real, but the app was not.

How to protect yourself:

  • Always check the app publisher and permissions before clicking “Accept.”
  • If unsure, cancel and report to IT Security.
